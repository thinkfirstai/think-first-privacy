Privacy Policy for Think First

Last Updated: July 3, 2025

Think First is a Chrome extension that helps users stay mindful while using AI tools by displaying playful GIFs during AI response generation.

What We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data. All preferences (like which GIFs are active) are saved locally in your browser.

Permissions

We request access to specific AI sites (like ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot) only to detect streaming activity. No content is read or stored.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit any user data to third parties.

Personally Identifiable Information

Think First does not collect or handle any personally identifiable information (PII), such as names, email addresses, IP addresses, or browsing history.

Contact

If you have questions, reach out to us at thinkfirstai@gmail.com.

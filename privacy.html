Privacy Policy for Think First

Last Updated: July 2, 2025

Think First is a Chrome extension that helps users stay mindful while using AI tools by displaying playful GIFs during AI response generation.

What We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data. All preferences (e.g. which GIFs are active) are saved locally in your browser’s storage.

Permissions

We request permission to access specific AI platforms (e.g., chat.openai.com, gemini.google.com) only to detect when they are streaming responses. No content from those pages is collected or stored.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at thinkfirstai@gmail.com.
